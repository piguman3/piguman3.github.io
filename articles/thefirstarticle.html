The first article on this thing

So, I should probably explain the existance of this site, shouldn't I?

A while back I discovered mitxela.com, the amazing blog about plotting, printers and really tiny LED earrings, and the also amazing bettermotherfuckingwebsite.com, a sequel to motherfuckingwebsite.com, now with 7 declarations of CSS.

When I looked at these sites, especially the second, I thought to myself, "What if I made my own website?"

I know some amount of HTML and the such, it isn't super hard to make a github pages site, and I can always learn from other websites to make one, so... Why not?

This website is essentially just a bunch of assets from different websites thrown together in a blender. The CSS for the body of the website was taken from bettermotherfuckingwebsite, the styling for the articles' div was done the same as mitxela.

Anything that isn't just stolen from other websites was done by just reading some tutorials on W3Schools, such as the "code styling".
